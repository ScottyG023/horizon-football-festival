Format

  • › 7-a-side across every age group.
  • › 18-minute games, played straight through with no halftime.
  • › 18 teams per age group, split into group-stage pools on day one.
  • › Day two: top group plays for Gold, middle for Silver, bottom for Bronze.

Eligibility

  • › Open to clubs, academies and individual teams.
  • › Players must meet age eligibility for their entered age group as of 31 Dec 2026.
  • › Squad sizes typically 9–12 players (7 on the pitch + rolling subs).
  • › Teams from anywhere in Australia (or beyond) welcome.
