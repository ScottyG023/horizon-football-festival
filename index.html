The Festival

The ultimate football experience.

High-quality competition, professional standards, and a genuine passion for the game — the right environment to inspire players to grow beyond what they thought possible.

Hosted by Manly United, the four-day festival provides teams of players aged U9–U13 the opportunity to accelerate their development in a well-structured tournament environment in one of Sydney's leading facilities — Cromer Park.

More than a series of fixtures, the Horizon Football Festival delivers a complete experience. Top-class facilities, a well-structured schedule, and an authentic tournament atmosphere — a chance for players to test themselves, embrace new challenges, and create lasting memories with their teammates.

"The right environment can inspire players to grow beyond what they thought possible."

— Vicki Halpin, CEO, Manly United FC

The Destination

More than a tournament. A weekend on the Northern Beaches.

Cromer Park sits at the edge of one of Australia's great coastal stretches. Top-class football by day, beach walks and ferry rides by afternoon — Manly is just 15 minutes from the pitch.

For interstate and overseas teams, the festival is a chance to make a trip of it. Sydney's beaches, restaurants, and the iconic Manly ferry are all on your doorstep.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who can enter the festival? +

Registration is open to clubs, academies, and individual teams. Teams from anywhere in Australia (or beyond) are welcome.

What age groups are available? +

Mixed: U9, U10, U11, U12. Girls: U10/U11 combined and U12/U13 combined. Days 1–2 (Fri 2 & Sat 3 Oct) host U9, U10 Mixed and U10/U11 Girls. Days 3–4 (Sun 4 & Mon 5 Oct, NSW Labour Day long weekend) host U11, U12 Mixed and U12/U13 Girls.

How many games will my team play? +

A minimum of 10 matches across two days. Day one is a full group stage; day two is the Bronze, Silver, or Gold finals group based on day-one placing.

What happens if it rains? +

All games are played on full synthetic pitches at Cromer Park. The festival proceeds rain, hail or shine — no weather cancellations.

What is the entry fee? +

Entry fee details will be confirmed shortly. Register your team's interest now and we'll send full pricing and inclusions as soon as they're released.

How do we register? +

Visit our registration page, or email tournaments@manlyunitedfc.com.au with any questions.
